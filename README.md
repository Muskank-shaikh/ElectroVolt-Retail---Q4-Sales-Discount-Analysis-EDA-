Although total revenue reached $907.75K, significant revenue remained unrealized due to:

5% smartphone discount failed to increase demand meaningfully

Old-stock laptops outperformed several new arrivals

Physical stores were the most reliable revenue channel

Accessories showed bundling potential but were underutilized
